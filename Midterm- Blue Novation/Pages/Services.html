Water skiing

Water skiing is a surface water sport in which an individual is pulled behind a boat or a cable ski installation over a body of water, skimming the surface on two skis or one ski.

Kayaking

Kayaking is the use of a kayak for moving across water. It is distinguished from canoeing by the sitting position of the paddler and the number of blades on the paddle.

Rowing

Rowing, sometimes referred to as crew in the United States. It involves propelling a boat on water using oars. By pushing against the water with an oar, a force is generated to move the boat.
